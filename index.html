The Problem

Manual LDIF prep breaks
at the worst moments

Directory admins hit the same painful edge cases on every bulk operation. LDIF Builder eliminates them.

⚠️
Syntax errors fail silently
Building LDIF by hand leads to attribute and formatting mistakes that only surface during import — hours after you've already moved on.
🔁
No repeatable process
Teams need a standardized path for add/replace/remove at scale. Not a one-off script someone wrote three years ago that nobody understands.
📋
Duplicates cause import failures
Master sheets often include entries that already exist. Catching them manually is tedious and unreliable under any kind of pressure.
