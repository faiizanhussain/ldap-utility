How Duplicates Are Identified

We compare the Distinguished Name (DN) of each entry in your Master Sheet with all entries in your Extract Sheet (from LDAP Explorer). When a DN from your Master Sheet matches a DN in the Extract Sheet, it's marked as a duplicate. This means the entry already exists in your LDAP directory and can be removed to avoid re-creating existing objects.
